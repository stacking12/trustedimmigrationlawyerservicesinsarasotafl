Our Services

  • Green Card Application Help

  • Family-Based Immigration Support

    • Family is everything, and keeping families together is at the heart of our practice. We offer dedicated family-based immigration support, assisting clients with spousal visas, petitions for parents and children, sibling petitions, and fiancé(e) visas.   • Citizenship And Naturalization Assistance

    • Becoming a U.S. citizen is a milestone that opens new doors of opportunity. We provide thorough citizenship and naturalization assistance, helping eligible applicants complete the N-400 application, prepare for the civics and English tests, and navigate the naturalization interview with confidence.

  • Marriage Green Card Attorney

    • A marriage-based green card involves a multi-step process that can be complex and time-consuming. As your trusted marriage green card attorney, we help couples document the legitimacy of their marriage, complete required USCIS forms, and prepare for the adjustment of status or consular processing interview.

  • Deportation Defense Lawyer

    • Facing removal or deportation is one of the most frightening experiences an immigrant can go through. Our skilled deportation defense lawyers will immediately review your case, identify all available forms of relief, and represent you aggressively in immigration court to fight for your right to stay in the United States.

  • Asylum Application Assistance

    • If you have fled your home country due to persecution or a well-founded fear of harm, you may be eligible for asylum in the United States. We offer professional asylum application assistance, helping you build a compelling case with supporting evidence and guiding you through the asylum interview or immigration court hearing.

  • Visa Petition Services

    • Whether you are applying for a work visa, student visa, or nonimmigrant visa, our team offers expert visa petition services to help ensure your application is correctly filed and strongly supported. We handle a wide variety of visa categories including H-1B, L-1, O-1, B-1/B-2, and more.

  • Immigration Appeals Representation

    • A denial is not always the end of the road. If your immigration case has been denied, our attorneys provide experienced immigration appeals representation before the Board of Immigration Appeals (BIA) and federal circuit courts, fighting to overturn unfair decisions and secure the outcome you deserve.

  • Adjustment Of Status Attorney

    • If you are already in the United States and wish to apply for lawful permanent residence without leaving the country, our adjustment of status attorneys will help you determine your eligibility, compile the required documentation, and guide you through every stage of the I-485 filing process.

  • Employment-Based Immigration Guidance

    • For foreign nationals seeking to work in the United States and employers looking to hire international talent, we provide comprehensive employment-based immigration guidance. From labor certification (PERM) to EB-1, EB-2, and EB-3 visa petitions, we help both employees and employers navigate the employment immigration process efficiently.
